Follow that shell!

February 27 marked a significant milestone in our shell recycling and oyster reef restoration efforts. We transported over 11,000 pounds of recycled oyster shells from the Yarmouth transfer station to A.R.C. Hatchery in Dennis. There, they will age for a year before we use them in future restoration projects. We did this as part of the Yarmouth Shellfish Collective. That group includes:

  • Mass Oyster Project
  • A.R.C. Hatchery
  • Friends of Bass River
  • The Yarmouth Division of Natural Resources
  • The Yarmouth Division of Public Works

Find out more about our work in Yarmouth and follow that shell at our website.
