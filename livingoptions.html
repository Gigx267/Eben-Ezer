Elim – Rehabilitative Care

Eben Ezer Lutheran Care Center offers comprehensive rehabilitation services including physical, occupational and speech therapy. These services can help to restore life functions and alleviate pain in those affected by stroke, injury, chronic pain, neurological and musculoskeletal disorders, and other health challenges.

Learn More

Evergreens and Sommerlyst - Memory Care

Restore function and get back to independent life with comprehensive therapy at Elim Rehab. Physical, occupational and speech therapy meet your every need with state-of-the-art, person-focused care. Just a little assistance needed for a short period of time while recovering strength for more independent living.

Learn More

Madsen And Deaconess - Long Term Care

Eben Ezer’s skilled nursing center is staffed with professional caregivers; nurses and certified nurse assistants. Neighbors are evaluated regularly to provide as much independence or attention as is needed. Private living spaces offer all the comforts of home.

Learn More

Dahms Talton – Assisted Living

The Pines Assisted Living is located in our newly constructed Dahms-Talton Building. These apartments are Medicaid certified and provide a resort-like environment that offers personalized services for individual needs.

Learn More

Arbor – Assisted Living

The Arbor is a licensed Assisted Living Residence and is for residents who want independent living that combines privacy with the joy of neighbors and the security of staff in the building 24 hours per day.

Learn More



Technology In The Home – Lifeline

Eben Ezer’s Lifeline Program is a personal response system which links a person from their home to a 24-hour assistant at the push of a button.

Learn More

Tabor Apartments – Independent Living

Tabor Apartments is a low-income 30-unit, recently renovated, apartment building of independent living. Twenty-three apartments are one-bedroom units and seven are studio apartments, some with a paneled wall between the living area and the bedroom. Tabor is physically attached by a walkway to Eben Ezer Lutheran Care Center. Rent is based on a HUD formula.
