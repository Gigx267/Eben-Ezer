About Us

Assisted Living, Skilled Nursing and & Memory Care

Eben Ezer has been a vital part of Morgan County for over 100 years. Our expert, dedicated team of caregiving professionals provide the quality care and services that older adults need to stay happy and healthy. Anchored in our faith-based roots and mission-driven, our strong and thriving neighbors tell our story.

For over a century, Eben Ezer has been a cornerstone of Morgan County, dedicated to creating an environment where life thrives. Rooted in faith and driven by a mission to empower older adults, we believe in fostering independence, health, and a fulfilling life. At Eben Ezer, we provide a continuum of care, encompassing Assisted Living, Skilled Nursing, and Memory Care, all conveniently located on one campus. This allows us to tailor our support to the unique needs of each individual, maximizing their well-being and autonomy. Our dedicated team of expert caregivers delivers compassionate, professional care, ensuring our residents, whom we fondly call "neighbors," live with dignity and joy.

Our Commitment:
Holistic Care: We understand that aging is a journey, and we provide comprehensive support that addresses physical, emotional, and spiritual needs. Rehabilitative Excellence: Our Elim Rehab center offers state-of-the-art physical, occupational, and speech therapy, empowering individuals to regain their independence and return to their daily lives. Community and Connection: We foster a vibrant community where residents, families, and caregivers connect, creating a supportive and enriching environment. Expansion and Growth: We are excited to announce our recent expansion, acquiring four additional assisted living communities in northeast Colorado. This allows us to extend our commitment to providing exceptional care and quality services to a wider community.

Our Vision:
We envision a community where every aging adult has the opportunity to live a fulfilling, meaningful, and purposeful life. We are committed to providing the choices and loving support necessary for our neighbors to live life their way.

Our Story:
Our story is told through the thriving lives of our neighbors, the dedication of our professional team, and the unwavering support of our faith-based roots. We are Eben Ezer, where life happens, and where assistance meets quality care for the well-being of everyone we serve.

Our Core Values

Guiding Principles at Eben Ezer

At Eben Ezer, our values are more than what we do, it’s who we are. They serve as a driving foundation for quality care, decision making, and service. We are committed to fostering an environment that prioritizes well-being, accountability, trust, reputation, and stewardship.

Well-Being:

We value wholeness by supporting Identity, Growth, Spirituality, Choice, Security, Connectedness, Meaning, and Joy. We strive to create a nurturing environment where all aspects of well-being are honored and cultivated.

Trust:

We value integrity and reliability in our relationships. Building trust through consistent and ethical behavior is fundamental to our interactions.

Stewardship:

We value the cultivation of time, talent, and treasure. We are committed to responsibly managing and utilizing our resources to benefit our community.
